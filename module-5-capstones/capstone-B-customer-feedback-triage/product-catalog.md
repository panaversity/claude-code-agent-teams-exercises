# CloudTask Product Catalog

## Company Overview

CloudTask is a project management SaaS platform for mid-market teams (20-200 employees). We help teams plan, track, and deliver projects with a focus on simplicity and collaboration.

**Founded**: 2021
**ARR**: $12M
**Customers**: 850 companies
**Employees**: 65

## Products

### CloudTask Core (Included in all plans)

- Project boards (Kanban and list views)
- Task management (assign, due dates, priorities, labels)
- Team workspaces
- File attachments (up to 100MB per file)
- Basic reporting (project status, task completion rates)
- Mobile app (iOS and Android)

### CloudTask Pro ($15/user/month)

Everything in Core, plus:

- Custom fields and templates
- Time tracking
- Advanced reporting and dashboards
- Guest access for clients
- Integrations (Slack, GitHub, Google Drive, Zapier)
- API access

### CloudTask Enterprise ($30/user/month)

Everything in Pro, plus:

- SSO (SAML 2.0)
- Audit logs
- Advanced permissions (project-level, field-level)
- Priority support (4-hour SLA)
- Custom integrations
- Dedicated account manager

# Competitor Profile: Fresh Paws

## Overview

- **Founded**: 2023
- **Headquarters**: San Francisco, CA
- **Funding**: Series A ($12M from Sequoia)
- **Revenue**: ~$4M ARR (estimated 2025, growing fast)
- **Employees**: 35

## Products & Services

- Fresh, refrigerated meals delivered weekly
- AI-powered diet planning based on pet health data
- Integration with popular pet health apps
- Vet-approved recipes with named ingredients
- Both dogs and cats

## Strengths

- First-mover in AI diet planning for pets
- Premium positioning with high margins (65% gross margin)
- Strong tech team (ex-Google, ex-Instacart engineers)
- Highest customer satisfaction scores in category (NPS 72)
- Growing 15% month-over-month

## Weaknesses

- Only available in SF Bay Area and Los Angeles currently
- Requires refrigeration (limits shipping range)
- High price point limits addressable market
- No dry food option for budget-conscious pet owners
- Small team means limited customer support hours (9am-5pm PT)

## Pricing

- Small dog plan: $89/week
- Medium dog plan: $125/week
- Large dog plan: $165/week
- Cat plan: $72/week
- Average order value: $115

## Recent Moves

- Expanding to Seattle and Portland in Q1 2026
- Beta testing a pet health monitoring wearable
- Partnership with Blue Cross pet insurance for dietary recommendations
- Exploring freeze-dried options to solve shipping range problem

# Competitor Profile: Acme Pet Co

## Overview

- **Founded**: 2018
- **Headquarters**: Austin, TX
- **Funding**: Series C ($45M total raised)
- **Revenue**: ~$28M ARR (estimated 2025)
- **Employees**: 120

## Products & Services

- Premium dry and wet food subscription boxes
- Custom meal plans based on breed, age, and weight
- Free shipping on orders over $50
- 30-day satisfaction guarantee

## Strengths

- Strong brand recognition in the subscription pet food space
- Established supply chain with 3 distribution centers (TX, OH, WA)
- High customer retention (78% after 12 months)
- Active social media presence (180K Instagram followers)
- Partnership with 200+ veterinarians for referrals

## Weaknesses

- Limited to dry and wet food only -- no fresh/refrigerated option
- Delivery window is 3-5 business days (no same-day or next-day)
- Website UX is dated (last major redesign was 2022)
- No cat food line -- dogs only
- Customer service response time averaging 48 hours

## Pricing

- Starter plan: $45/month (1 dog, dry food only)
- Premium plan: $78/month (1 dog, dry + wet food)
- Family plan: $120/month (up to 3 dogs)
- Average order value: $72

## Recent Moves

- Launched breed-specific formulas in Q3 2025
- Hired new CTO from Chewy in January 2026
- Testing same-day delivery in Austin and Dallas
- Filed trademark for "PetGenome" (likely DNA-based nutrition product)
